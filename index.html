About The Event

About Cursors

Cursors is the annual techfest from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) of Anil Neerukonda Institue of Technology and Sciences (ANITS). Get ready for the exciting experiece of learning, collaboration, and fun! Cursors brings together students, professionals, and technology enthusiasts from all over the country. With a variety of events and activities ranging from codeathons and hackathons to workshops and tech talks, there is something for everyone interested in technology and innovation.

Where

Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), Anil Neerukonda Institue of Technology and Sciences (ANITS).

When

Thursday and Friday
2 - 3 March
